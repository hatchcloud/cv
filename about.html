About me

As a designer i have experience collaborating with product teams to create UI and UX designs. I had lead UX/UI design, conduct usability tests, and maintain hand-off documentation working close with development. I have experience working with clients such as Banco Pichincha, Cargo Expreso Logistics, Banco Nacional and others. I've worked as a Travel Retail Designer for Grupo Britt, overseeing all the design process for many restaurants and management schedule.

Estudié Arquitectura como primer carrera y trabajé logística y diseño retail en aeropuertos por varios años. Hace unos años empecé a trabajar con productos digitales de diferentes escalas. También me gusta dibujar, o disfrutar de un buen café.
